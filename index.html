Overview

What the framework is and why it matters.

Evidence-centric

Claim verification is treated as structured reasoning: parse ‚Üí retrieve ‚Üí assess ‚Üí decide, with explicit citations for every decision.

Multi-agent

Specialized agents handle retrieval, perception, verification, and deliberation. Debate improves robustness and reduces single-model failure modes.

Tool-augmented

Integrates web search, VQA, time detection, entity grounding, and forgery cues under a single orchestrated pipeline.

Use cases: misinformation monitoring, newsroom verification, social media moderation, and decision support for analysts.

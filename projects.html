Architecture, modules, and how to reproduce experiments.

Architecture

  1. Claim parsing: extract entities, time, location, and sub-claims.
  2. Stage-1 evidence: retrieval + tool calls per subtask (text/image/match).
  3. Veracity estimation: calibrated confidence per modality/subtask.
  4. Debate fusion: skeptic vs supporter + judge aggregation.

Modules

  • Orchestrator: planning, budgets, stopping rules.
  • Retrieval: Google/HybridRAG/Wikipedia, citation tracking.
  • Perception: optional forgery cues, VQA, time detection.
  • Decision: uncertainty-aware fusion + logs.
